House Call Kombucha

Medium: Three printed labels on bottles

The goal of this project was to design a healthy beverage for someone in their late 20s to early that is very active and is always on the go. My partner and I decided to do research into Kombucha, a fermented tea with several health benefits. Because Kombucha was originally designed for healing purposes, we decided to reference old medicinal design of the 1920s. We wanted to do simple illustrations with watercolor and wanted to incorporate a measuring device to encourage people to continue drinking. Similarly, we chose fruits with specific benefits such as for energy, memory, and fiber.
