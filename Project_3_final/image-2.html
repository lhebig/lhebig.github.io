Dew Tour 2017

Medium: Three printed posters and promotional video

For this project, I created a campaign for Mountain Dew’s “Dew Tour 2017,” a snowboard/skiing competition that takes place in Breckenridge, Colorado. I started by designing three static iterations of the same poster and then developed a motion graphic to pair with the video. I created a design that appeals to the audience that attends the Dew Tour. For the motion graphic, I specifically chose stock music that fits with the types of bands that perform at this event.
