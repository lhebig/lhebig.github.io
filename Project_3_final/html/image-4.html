Bodoni Typeface

Medium: Print magazine (glossy paper)

The goal of this project was to create a book explaining the history of a typeface of our choosing. Being a big history buff, I was drawn to Bodoni because of its elegant background and, of course, the beauty of the typeface itself (fun fact: Bodoni was originally designed for the nobility). Because Vogue originally used Bodoni for its logo, I decided to highlight this concept and create a magazine similar to Vogue.
