Spirit Animal

Medium: Three printed labels on cans with carrying box (with cutouts)

When looking at current alcohol packaging, there is no company that is able to bring iconic bar drinks to the household. College students enjoy the taste of these iconic beverages but often times do not want to spend the money. Therefore, my partner and I saw a need for a company that unified the most iconic alcoholic beverages by creating Spirit Animal. Spirit Animal is a company that takes health into consideration and brings the taste of your favorite cocktail beverages to your household. We chose Mojito, Sangria, and Moscow Mule because these are the most iconic drinks. Similarly, these drinks have a unique history behind each, which is why we specifically chose an animal for each drink. For example, since Sangria was originally created in Spain, we chose a bull since the bull is Spain’s national animal.
