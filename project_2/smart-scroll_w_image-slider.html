Pick your Shoe

<< >>

Competition shoes are good for quick, tempo runs. Lightweight, flexible, moderate cushion.

Trail shoes are good for running on rugged terrain. Lightweight, stable, less cushioning, extra-traction.

Cushion shoes are good for long runs on flat, hard terrain. Flexible, extra-cushioning, stable.
